Hello! My name is Samantha Celek. I am a Senior Communications major and Art minor at Coker College. I am from Charleston, SC. I will attain a Bachelor's of Art in Communications in May 2015.

I recently studied abroad in Florence, Italy and found my passion for art and travel while studying there for three months. I took Web Design I, Visual Communication Design, and History of Photography in Italy. I am currently enrolled in Web Design II and Graphic Design I at Coker.

I also play volleyball for my college and enjoy working out, hanging out with friends and family, and watching movies. I enjoy pushing myself out of my comfort zone and trying new things.

Thanks for viewing my portfolio page! Please feel free to contact me if you have questions or are interested in my work. My references are available upon request.

Tesini Wine Labels
February 2014

I made these wine labels for my course in Florence, Italy while studying abroad. The class was to create two different wine labels, one for a brut and one for a rose. The labels were for the new Vinicollina Tesini wine company. The owner of the company, Dr. Tesini, was to pick the label suitable for his two products. Dr. Tesini ended up picking my Lila label for his rose. I am very honored to represent the company and Italian vineyard with my work.
Designed with: Adobe Illustrator CC 2014
